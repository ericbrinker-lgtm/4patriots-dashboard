We did this holdout test, as we would with any major channel, to find out whether the business impact from this channel is more, less, or exactly the same as the Tableau sales. And we found much more overall sales impact than just DG's own last-click sales: Facebook, MKT, untracked sales, and Google Search/PMAX all improved sales and revenue in the areas where we had Demand Gen. Some of the channels spent more to chase this return (below you can choose whether or not to factor this into the iROAS). Is the halo effect real? Our geo test isn't the only tool to see it: Our MTA says that recently an average 12.8 days passed between DG first click and last click to a sale. Google Ads says that on average DG has 24 views/clicks for every last-click sale. But the MTA only assigns about 10% more revenue to DG on the most generous click-based model. That means either that the halo effect largely comes from VIEWS and only somewhat from CLICKS, or other channels' clicks claim some of that later credit. Either way that means DG's halo isn't in a void: the halo happens on other channels. This is why we may be able to consider a Tableau ROAS of 0.60x as our lower target.
