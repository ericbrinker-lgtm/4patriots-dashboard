Notes

Matched Markets — control is a hand-picked set of DMAs matched to treatment on pre-period daily orders (MAPE and correlation). Primary model for headline iROAS.

Synthetic Control — control is a weighted portfolio of donor DMAs that reconstruct the treatment counterfactual. This view shows any DMA with a nonzero weight; weight magnitude is not encoded.

Whole Country — treatment DMAs compared against the full rest-of-country. Directional validator; no hand-picked control set.
