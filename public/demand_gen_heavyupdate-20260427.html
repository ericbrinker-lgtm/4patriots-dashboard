Show the math · how each bar is computed

Orders iROAS = (Incremental Orders × Control-cell AOV) ÷ Incremental Spend. Revenue iROAS = Incremental GP Revenue ÷ Incremental Spend. Revenue figures are already gross profit — no margin multiplier applied.

Incremental orders = treatment orders − (control orders × scale factor). MM scale factor this run: 1.1958. Single orders SF applied universally to orders, revenue, spend, and sessions.
